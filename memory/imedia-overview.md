# iMedia Overview

## Company Profile
- **Type:** Digital media conglomerate
- **Headquarters:** Petaling Jaya (with legacy offices in Penang)
- **Staff:** ~200
- **Primary Revenue:** Advertising (brands, media agencies, integrated campaigns)
- **COO:** Ken

## Business Units

### 1. Digital Media Brands (Core Business)
Grouped by language and vertical.

**Mass/Lifestyle:**
- Malay: Oh Media, Beautiful Nara, TheReporter, EZLokal, KS Lagi
- Chinese: Goody25, Moretify
- English: GoodyMY, Weirdkaya

**Tech:**
- English: Technave (main)
- Malay: Technave BM
- Chinese: Technave CN

**Automotive (Drive2Digital group):**
- Chinese: Automachi, Auto123
- Malay: Mekanika, Careta

### 2. Influencer Agency (ITTIFY)
- Runs influencer campaigns across TikTok, IG, FB, XHS
- Also operates under Drive2Digital for automotive-focused campaigns

### 3. DOOH (Digital Out-of-Home)
- Digital billboard at Tun Razak
- iStadia: newly launched digital billboard ecosystem at Bukit Jalil National Stadium Area

### 4. Social Media Agency (iCANVAS)
- Manages social media for clients
- Smaller part of overall business
- Functions somewhat independently

### 5. Events
- Coffee in the Clouds (coffee rave)
- Layan Bawah Bintang (music event)
- The Social Serve (celebrity pickleball)

## Revenue Model
- Brand sponsorships for events
- Branded content (videos, social posts, articles) on digital brands
- Billboard advertising
- Influencer campaigns
- Integrated campaigns (cross-selling across the group)

## Key Clients
- FMCG brands
- Telcos
- Phone manufacturers
- Media agencies (Omnicom, IPG, Mindshare, etc.)

## Organizational Structure

| Department | Function |
|---|---|
| SALES | Frontline client meetings, brief acquisition |
| STRATEGY | Brief analysis, pitch deck creation |
